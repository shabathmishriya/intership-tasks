About the Internship

The Web Developer Internship at Cognifyz Technologies is a dynamic and immersive program designed to provide aspiring developers with hands-on experience in the fast-paced world of technology. Over the course of 12 weeks, interns will have the opportunity to work on real projects, collaborate with seasoned professionals, and build a strong portfolio that showcases their skills.

Overview

Join our dynamic team at Cognifyz Technologies and dive into the world of web development. Our internship program, lasting 3 months, offers you the chance to work on real-world projects, develop your skills, and receive mentorship from experienced developers.


Highlights

  • Gain real-world experience with live projects
  • Receive one-on-one mentorship from industry experts
  • Expand your professional network
  • Get hands-on training in modern web development technologies

Qualifications

  • Experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Familiarity with version control systems (e.g., Git)
  • Basic understanding of front-end frameworks (e.g., Bootstrap, React)
  • Passion for learning and a proactive attitude
