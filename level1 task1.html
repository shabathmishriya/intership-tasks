About Us

The Institute of Engineering & Technology is a constituent college under Srinivas University started with offering 4 years Bachelor Degree program B.Tech. in Computer Science Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Cyber Security & Cyber Forensics. The college offers 2 years Master Degree program M.Tech in Structural Engineering,Construction Management,Computer Science & Engineering,Industrial Nano- Biotechnology, and Artificial Intelligence & Virtual Reality. The college is located at Srinivas University Campus, Mukka, Mangaluru.
